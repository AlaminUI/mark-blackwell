SAND CITY REALTORS - Nicholas Tortorelli - LOCAL Visalia BAY REALTOR SINCE 2013

RESULTS THAT MOVE YOU!

If you're looking for honest and hard working local Sand City realtors who are constantly available, look no further. Nicholas Tortorelli’s goal is to make sure that, at the end of the day, his clients know he worked diligently to get the job done, and did everything in his power to protect their best interests.

Nicholas focuses on sales and rentals located on the Visalia Peninsula.

Nicholas is always looking for great homes for his clients as well as potential investments. His passion for the business and unrelenting work ethic are why he excels when it comes to listening to clients’ needs, and exceeding their objectives. Nicholas continually stays abreast of the ever-changing conditions in the real estate market, including everything from city planning department policy to zoning changes, to regional supply/demand shifts, and breaks it all down into very usable information which helps his clients to the fullest in making the right real estate decisions.

A born and raised son of California, Nicholas holds a Bachelors of Arts in Social and Behavioral Science from California State University Visalia Bay. His affinity for the Central Coast along with its rich history and architecture drew him to become a real estate broker in 2013.

Nick’s vast network, keen eye for detail, analytical mind and business ingenuity compliment his top-down understanding of all aspects of buying, selling, renting or investing in residential and commercial real estate.

- Nicholas Tortorelli
